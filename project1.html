ARCHITECTURAL THESIS

CONNEXUS: A MULTI MODAL TRANSIT HUB, VIJAYAWADA

S I T E

Site: Near Pandit Nehru Bus Station (PNBS), Vijayawada, Andhra Pradesh.

Coordinates: Approx. 16.509° N, 80.618° E

Client: Government Of Andhra Pradesh

Connectivity: Adjacent to Vijayawada Railway Station and proposed metro corridors

To design a futuristic, efficient, and user-centric Multimodal Transit Hub in Vijayawada that enhances interconnectivity between metro, rail, and bus services, while incorporating public and commercial spaces to support a Live-Work-Play urban lifestyle.

M A S T E R   P L A N

1. MRTS STATION

2. URBAN PLAZA

3. WATERFRONT PARKS

4. CITY BUS TERMINUS

5. M.L.C.P

6. SKYWALK

F L O O R   P L A N S

Ground Floor Plan:
The ground floor of the Connexus hub serves as the primary public interface, designed for comfort, orientation, and flow. It features a dual-entry system — one for general public use and another for staff and operational control.

First Floor Plan:
The first floor functions as the main concourse level, where passengers begin their pre-boarding process, combining paid and unpaid areas for smooth and secured transitions.

F L O O R   P L A N S

Second Floor:
The second floor functions as the first elevated platform level, accommodating both passenger movement and critical infrastructure. The layout is bifurcated into public transit and service-oriented zones.

Third Floor:
The third floor is a combination of second-tier passenger transit access and administrative/executive operations, ensuring seamless connectivity and institutional functionality.

F L O O R   P L A N S

Fourth to Seventh Floor:
These four upper floors are typical layouts designed to accommodate a blend of government departments and private corporate offices, fostering institutional synergy within the transit hub ecosystem.

Workstations:   432 (108 Each Floor)

Urban Plaza:
Recreational forecourt with kiosks. At the ground level, adjacent to the main public entry, the Urban Plaza acts as a vibrant recreational and social interface for users.

Kiosks:   14 (Includes Retail & Eateries)

M U L T I - L E V E L C A R P A R K I N G

A dedicated MLCP is proposed to manage vehicles from metro commuters, bus passengers, and visitors to nearby recreational areas.

Connectivity: A skywalk links the MLCP with the proposed metro, bus terminal, concourse, and recreational zones, ensuring seamless intermodal access.

Design Features:

  • Ramp Width: 7 m
  • Gradient: 1:10 (as per IRC/NBC)
  • Circulation: Separate entry/exit ramps for smooth flow

Configuration: Basement + 3 Floors + Rooftop

Capacity: 458 Cars and 1,000+ Two-Wheelers

SKYWALK:
A 624-meter elevated Skywalk Network is proposed to ensure safe, seamless, and comfortable pedestrian mobility across the Connexus Transit Hub and its surroundings.

Key Connections:

  • Vijayawada Railway Station
  • Government Hospital
  • VMC City Park
  • City Bus Terminus
  • Metro Station
  • PNBS Main Bus Stand
  • Multi-Level Car Parking (MLCP)

CITY BUS PORT:
The proposed City Bus Terminus at Connexus Transit Hub is a facility designed to handle 400 buses daily across 53 routes, strengthening intra-city connectivity.

Bus Bay: 18 (Upgraded from previous 10)

Accommodate: 90 - 100k Passengers daily
