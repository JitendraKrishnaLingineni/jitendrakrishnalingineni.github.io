D E S I G N I N G N A T U R E C O N N E C T I N G P E O P L E

S I T E

Site: PWD maidan, Maidan, Kolkata, West Bengal, India.

Coordinates: Approx. 22°33'23"N 88°20'43"E

Site Area: 7901 Sq.m (1.95 Acres)

Architecture: Victorian Style

The proposal transforms a vacant triangular plot in Kolkata into a multifunctional urban park. The design integrates various amenities for the people of Kolkata.

E X I S T I N G I S S U E S

The site lacks basic infrastructure such as seating, pathways, and pedestrian facilities, making it underutilized.

P R O P O S E D P A R K

The landscape strategy emphasizes greenery, shaded tree zones, and interconnected pathways, creating a balanced environment that supports recreation, leisure, and social interaction.
